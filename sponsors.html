About Pet Pleasers

Pet Pleasers was started in 1989, by my Mother, JoAnne Sandberg. I took over the business. Slowly. My Mother started with cats and dogs. I added the horses recently. I trained horses for years. It was a natural addition to my supplemental business. I have vet tech experience and can give medications and feed supplements. I will care for your babies like they are my own. I limit my workload for the animals' sake. I want all of them to have a wonderful experience while in my care.
